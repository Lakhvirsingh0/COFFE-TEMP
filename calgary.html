CALGARY

Calgary’s coffee scene is packed with independent cafés that focus on quality beans, expert roasting, and unique atmospheres. From espresso bars to cozy cafés, the city has something for every kind of coffee drinker. Whether you're looking for a smooth pour-over, a bold espresso, or a handcrafted latte, Calgary’s local coffee culture delivers.

This list highlights indie cafés, small-batch roasters, and specialty coffee shops across the city. Whether you're in Kensington, Inglewood, or the Beltline, these spots offer some of the best specialty coffee in Calgary.
