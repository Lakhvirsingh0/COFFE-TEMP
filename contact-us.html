CONTACT

Celebrating Canada’s Independent Coffee Culture

At Coffee Spotz, we’re always happy to hear from fellow coffee lovers, independent café owners, and anyone who appreciates a well-crafted cup of coffee. If you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions, feel free to reach out.

📧 Email: info@coffeespotz.com

Since we’re a digital coffee shop directory, we don’t have a physical office or phone line, but we’re always available by email.

Want to Get Your Coffee Shop Listed?

If you own or manage an independent coffee shop, small-batch roastery, or specialty café in Canada and would like to be featured on Coffee Spotz, we’d love to hear from you!

✔️ Be part of Canada’s go-to coffee directory

✔️ Increase your visibility among coffee lovers

✔️ Showcase your specialty drinks, unique atmosphere, and brewing methods

To apply for a listing, simply email us at info@coffeespotz.com with:

☕ Your café’s name, location, and website (if available)

☕ A short description of what makes your coffee shop special

☕ Your specialty drinks and coffee offerings

☕ Any additional details (pricing, seating, WiFi availability, etc.)

We carefully review all submissions to ensure they align with our mission of promoting authentic, high-quality independent coffee shops across Canada.
